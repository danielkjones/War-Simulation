We did a few test runs to understand the average amount of 
time that it takes to complete two types of turns:
    1. Typical turns (two cards, one beats other, no war)
    2. War turns (two cards match, two more are played per player for a win)


Time to Complete 10 Turns, No War:

-----------------------------------------------------------
|  Trail #  | Time TTC 10 Turns (s) |  AVG TTC 1 Turn (s) |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   1       |       34.02           |       3.40          |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   2       |       30.02           |       3.00          |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   3       |       27.60           |       2.76          |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   AVG     |       30.54           |       3.05          |
-----------------------------------------------------------


Time to Complete 10 Turns, All War:

-----------------------------------------------------------
|  Trail #  | Time TTC 10 Turns (s) |  AVG TTC 1 Turn (s) |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   1       |       142.14          |       14.21         |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   2       |       105.07          |       10.51         |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   3       |       111.03          |       11.10         |
-----------------------------------------------------------
|   AVG     |       119.41          |       11.94         |
-----------------------------------------------------------

Based on the very rudimental test data, a normal turn takes as average of 
3.05 seconds and a war turn takes on average 11.94 seconds.
